Common questions

Does it work without the internet?

Yes. Your emergency card is stored locally and can be displayed and spoken offline.

What about privacy?

We do not track you. Your data is encrypted and stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

Can responders use it quickly?

Absolutely — a simple scan opens your card; big text + audio highlight the critical details.
