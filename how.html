How it works

Three simple steps to make sure your voice is heard in an emergency.

1) Create your card

Add allergies, conditions, meds, ICE contacts, and care preferences (DNR/DNI, transfusion notes, donor status).

2) Link your tag

Use a wristband, keychain, sticker, or a phone widget. A quick scan opens your read‑only emergency view.

3) Share in seconds

ANERITA displays and voices the essentials — even offline — so responders act faster and safer.

Works offline

Your emergency card is stored on‑device. If there’s no signal, scanning still shows the essentials and can play the audio summary.
