About ANERITA

ANERITA is a patient‑first emergency identification system that shows — and speaks — the essentials when seconds matter. It works online or offline, in any country.

Mission

Help responders act faster and safer by giving them clear, consent‑driven information — allergies, conditions, meds, and care preferences — in seconds, in their language.

Why it matters

  • Clarity in chaos: emergency decisions are made fast. We surface what’s critical first.
  • Privacy by design: your data lives on your device unless you choose to share it.
  • Global by default: big text + voice + translations to bridge language barriers.

Safety & Privacy

  • On‑device first: emergency card works offline; optional cloud features later.
  • Zero tracking: no analytics cookies; we use privacy‑friendly telemetry only when you opt‑in.
  • Consent & control: you decide what shows on scan, and who sees what.

Compliance roadmap: UK GDPR baseline; HIPAA alignment for any U.S. hosting/integrations; ISO 27001 practices as we scale.

From a patient, for patients

ANERITA was created by a patient who experienced how hard it is to be heard during emergencies. This tool exists so your voice and wishes are clear when you can’t speak.
